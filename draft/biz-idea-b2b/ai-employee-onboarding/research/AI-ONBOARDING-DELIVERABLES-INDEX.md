## üéØ Key Insights at a Glance

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM:** $2.12B global onboarding SaaS market (growing 20%/year)
- **SAM:** $280-350M SMB-focused segment (North America)
- **SOM (Year 1):** $18-35M ARR (conservative 1-2% market capture)
- **Verdict:** STRONG PASS (18/20 scoring)

### Target ICP
- **Title:** VP People / Chief People Officer
- **Company Size:** 50-150 employees
- **Stage:** Series A-B startup
- **Budget:** $12-18K/year
- **Buying Committee:** 2 people (VP People + CFO)
- **Sales Cycle:** 60-90 days
- **Trigger Events:** Series B funding, high turnover, geographic expansion

### Pain Points (Quantified)
- **Time Wasted:** 15-20 hours/week on manual onboarding = $175K/year
- **Failed Hires:** 20% of new hires leave by 90 days = $420K/year
- **Compliance Risk:** I-9 audits, penalties = $10-50K/year exposure
- **Productivity Loss:** 3-week onboarding ramp = $180K/year
- **TOTAL ANNUAL COST:** $615K-800K per customer
- **POTENTIAL SAVINGS:** $231K/year with AI automation

### Competitive Gaps
1. **AI Document Intelligence** (I-9 auto-fill, 8+ hours saved per hire) ‚Äî No competitor doing this
2. **Integrated HR+IT Onboarding** (single dashboard vs BambooHR + 4 integrations) ‚Äî Rippling does it but $50K/year, too expensive
3. **SMB-First Pricing** ($8-12K/year vs Rippling's $50K) ‚Äî Clear price-performance gap
4. **Compliance Automation** (2025 I-9 form, audit trail, state-specific rules) ‚Äî No one doing comprehensive solution
5. **New Hire Experience** (mobile-first, AI chatbot, personalized onboarding) ‚Äî Rippling has UI, but not AI-driven

### MVP Feature Prioritization

**P0 (MVP - Months 1-3):**
- Digital onboarding checklist (3-4 weeks)
- Document collection + storage (3-4 weeks)
- I-9 compliance templates (2-3 weeks)
- Admin dashboard (2-3 weeks)
- Email + Slack notifications (2 weeks)
- Total effort: 14-17 weeks across team

**P1 (Phase 1 - Months 4-6):**
- AI document intelligence/OCR (4-6 weeks)
- AI chatbot (4-6 weeks)
- Payroll/HRIS integration (3-4 weeks)
- ATS integration (3-4 weeks)
- Additional integrations

**P2 (Phase 2+):**
- Predictive attrition scoring (6-8 weeks)
- SSO/SAML (Okta)
- Advanced compliance reporting
- Multi-location support

### Pricing Model

| Tier | Use Case | Price | Includes |
|------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | Early-stage (20-100 emp) | $500/mo + $2K setup = $8K ACV | Checklist, documents, I-9, dashboard |
| **Growth** | Growing (100-300 emp) | $1,200/mo = $14.4K ACV | Everything + AI features + integrations |
| **Enterprise** | Scaling (300+ emp) | Custom (typically $3K+/mo) | Everything + dedicated support |

**Free Trial:** 2 weeks, 5 new hires, no credit card (goal: 20-25% conversion)

### GTM Strategy
**Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Founder-Led Sales**
- Target: 5-8 customers
- Method: Cold email (100 prospects), warm intros, free trial
- Owner: Founder + part-time support
- Goal: Validate ICP, refine sales process

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Scaling**
- Target: 10-15 total customers
- Method: Hire AE, referral partners, content marketing
- Owner: AE + founder on strategic deals
- Goal: $150-225K ARR by year-end

**Phase 3 (Year 2): Expansion**
- Target: 50+ customers
- Method: Vertical expansion (healthcare, finance, retail)
- Owner: Sales team + AE per vertical
- Goal: $600K ARR, Series A readiness

### Development Roadmap (90 Days)

**Month 1 (Weeks 1-4):** Foundation + Core MVP
- Week 1-2: Project setup, auth, DB, frontend scaffolding
- Week 3-4: Onboarding checklist + document collection + I-9 templates
- Week 5: Admin dashboard + email/Slack notifications
- **Deliverable:** MVP core features, 5 beta customers, NPS > 40

**Month 2 (Weeks 5-8):** Integration & Polish
- Week 5: Slack bot + email customization + mobile optimization
- Week 6: ATS integration (Workable)
- Week 7: Audit logging + security + GDPR compliance
- Week 8: QA + bug fixes + beta customer onboarding
- **Deliverable:** 3-5 paying beta customers, 70% test coverage

**Month 3 (Weeks 9-12):** AI Features & Launch
- Week 9: AI document intelligence (OCR, I-9 auto-fill)
- Week 10: AI chatbot (LLM integration)
- Week 11: Marketing (landing page, pricing, help center)
- Week 12: Public launch (soft + Product Hunt)
- **Deliverable:** Public MVP, 5-10 paying customers, $10-15K MRR
