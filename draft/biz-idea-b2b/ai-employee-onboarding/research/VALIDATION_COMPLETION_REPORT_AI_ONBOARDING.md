## Critical Findings: 7 Weaknesses Identified

### Weakness #1: ACV Pricing Power Crisis (HIGH)
**Evidence**: BambooHR includes onboarding at $7.2K-18K; your product is onboarding-only at $12K
**Impact**: Pricing power unproven ‚Üí ACV may drop to $6K point-solution pricing
**Fix**: Need ROI calculator showing $50K+ savings vs competitor bundles

### Weakness #2: CAC Undercosting (HIGH)
**Evidence**: Founder labor costs ~$3-5K per customer; true blended CAC ~$4-5K
**Impact**: Payback looks good (4.8 mo) but Year 1 cash burn = -$100K
**Fix**: Plan for $200-300K Series Seed or extend runway

### Weakness #3: Churn Assumption Collapse (CRITICAL)
**Evidence**: <3% target unrealistic; SMB HR SaaS = 3.5-5.7% monthly
**Impact**: At 4% churn, LTV = $25K (not $60K); LTV:CAC = 5:1 (not 10:1)
**Fix**: Implement CS playbook from Day 1, target 4% monthly or model fails

### Weakness #4: Competitive Displacement (HIGH)
**Evidence**: BambooHR/Rippling can add onboarding feature in 12 months ($300-500K cost)
**Impact**: 18-month window before feature parity ‚Üí market access lost
**Fix**: Build defensible IP/partnerships/community moat by Q4 2026

### Weakness #5: TAM Penetration (MEDIUM)
**Evidence**: 30K tech companies in USA; targeting 25-35 Year 1 = 0.09% penetration
**Impact**: Need 5-10% penetration to reach $1M ARR ‚Üí TAM too narrow
**Fix**: Expand to healthcare, finance, manufacturing in Year 2

### Weakness #6: Founder Sales Ceiling (MEDIUM)
**Evidence**: Solo founder can close max 35 customers/year (5-8 demos/week, 2-3 mo sales cycle)
**Impact**: Cannot scale to 100+ customers without hire ‚Üí need co-founder or funding
**Fix**: Hire SDR/AE by Month 12 after hitting $200K+ revenue

### Weakness #7: NRR Assumptions (MEDIUM)
**Evidence**: 90% NRR requires <2% churn + expansion revenue (point solutions have neither)
**Impact**: Realistic NRR = 70-80% (not 90%), limiting growth
**Fix**: Plan expansion features (payroll, HRIS) for land-and-expand
