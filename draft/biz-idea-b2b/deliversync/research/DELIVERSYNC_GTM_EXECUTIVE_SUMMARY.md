## Critical Success Factors (Do These, Win. Skip These, Fail.)

### 1. **Founder Availability: 25+ Hours/Week (Months 1-6)**
- **Why:** First customer is product-market fit validation. Only founder has credibility.
- **Risk:** If founder can't do this, GTM fails immediately.
- **Mitigation:** If unavailable, hire interim sales consultant or advisor.

### 2. **ICP Precision: 40%+ Outreach to True VP Operations/Ops Director**
- **Why:** Wrong ICP kills CAC (burns through prospects).
- **Action:** Build ZoomInfo/Apollo list of 500 targeted firms. Validate fit before campaign.
- **Flag:** If response rate <8%, ICP is wrong. Pause and recalibrate.

### 3. **Trial-to-Paid Conversion: 60%+**
- **Why:** Makes or breaks sales velocity. If <40%, cycle extends to 12+ weeks.
- **Action:** Day 15 check-in call. Day 20 "aha moment" measurement (50+ hours logged).
- **Flag:** If <50%, product/onboarding needs work before scaling.

### 4. **Sales Cycle Discipline: 70%+ Close Within 8 Weeks**
- **Why:** Long cycles = high founder effort per deal. Need velocity.
- **Action:** Track cycle length weekly. Escalate trials >60 days.
- **Flag:** If average >70 days, objection handling or product clarity issue.

### 5. **Referral Loop by Month 6: 20%+ of New Opportunities**
- **Why:** Enables transition to Phase 2. Reduces CAC.
- **Action:** Collect customer testimonials. Build case studies. Referral program ($500 credit).
- **Flag:** If <10% referrals by month 6, customer satisfaction issue.
