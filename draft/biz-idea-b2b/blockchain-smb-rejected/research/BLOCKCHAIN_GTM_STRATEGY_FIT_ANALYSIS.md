## OPPORTUNITY 1: SMART CONTRACT TESTING SAAS
### ACV: $18-35K | TAM: $500M-1B

### Market Context
- **Buyer Profile:** DevOps engineers, lead developers at 20-200 person blockchain teams
- **Problem:** Foundry is 10x faster but 10x harder to learn; Hardhat is slow (15min CI pipelines)
- **Market Gap:** No tool offers Foundry speed + Hardhat UX + enterprise support
- **Competitive Landscape:** Foundry (OSS, free), Hardhat (free, slow), No commercial solution winning market
- **CAC Benchmarks:** $500-1.5K (developer tools), LTV:CAC target 3:1+

**Score: 9/10**

**PROS:**
- ‚úÖ **Buyer Tech Savviness**: DevOps/lead devs are digitally native; expect self-serve UX

- ‚úÖ **Low CAC Opportunity**: GitHub + Product Hunt + developer community = $300-800 CAC

- ‚úÖ **2-3 demo calls/week**: Handles expansion revenue without scaling sales

**CONS:**
- ‚ùå **Free Alternative Exists**: Foundry is OSS and free; must justify $18-35K premium (speed alone may not be enough)

- ‚ùå **Developer Attention**: DevOps are busy; trial abandonment rate could be high (30-50%)
