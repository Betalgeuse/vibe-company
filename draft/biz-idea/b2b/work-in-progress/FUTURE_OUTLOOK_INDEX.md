### Scoring Formula

**Base (Market Trajectory):**
- Definite growth = 5/10
- Modest growth = 3.5/10
- Shrinking = 2/10

**Add (Technology Certainty):** +2 pts (proven) to -1 pt (breakthrough needed)

**Add (Regulatory Outlook):** +2 pts (favorable) to -2 pts (hostile)

**Add (Competitive Feasibility):** +2 pts (easy win) to -1 pt (moat unbreakable)

**Adjust (Strategy Clarity):** +2 pts (clear plan) to -2 pts (indefinite & bad)

**Result:** Floor 0, Ceiling 10

### Key Insight: Definite vs Indefinite

- ‚úÖ **Definite:** "We will be #2 algo trading platform for Python devs by 2027"
- ‚úÖ **Clear:** Market growing, path proven, strategy defined
- ‚ùå **Indefinite:** "We will disrupt financial services" (multiple paths, unclear which wins)
- ‚ùå **Unbeatable:** "We will compete with Chainalysis" (regulatory moat unbreakable)
