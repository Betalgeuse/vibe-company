## üìà Scoring Logic

### How Scores Are Calculated

**Base: Market Trajectory** (Foundation: Does the market exist and grow?)
- Definite growing market = 5/10 baseline
- Modest/indefinite growth = 3.5/10 baseline
- Shrinking market = 2/10 baseline

**Add: Technology Certainty** (Can this be built?)
- All tech proven, clear path = +2 pts
- Tech mostly proven, some unknowns = +1 pt
- Breakthrough innovation needed = 0 pts

**Add: Regulatory Outlook** (Will government help or hurt?)
- Favorable or neutral = +2 pts
- Uncertain/mixed = +1 pt
- Hostile/tightening = -2 pts

**Add: Competitive Feasibility** (Can you win?)
- Clear differentiation path = +2 pts
- Difficult but possible = +1 pt
- Incumbent moat unbreakable = -1 pt

**Adjust: Strategic Clarity** (Do you know what you're doing?)
- Clear 10-year strategy = +2 pts
- Unclear strategy, multiple paths = -1 to -2 pts
- Strategy conflicts with fundamentals = -2 pts

**Floor at 0, Ceiling at 10**

### Example: #1 Algo Trading
- Base (definite growing market): 5
- + Tech certainty: 2 (all proven)
- + Regulatory: 1.5 (favorable now, risk 2027+)
- + Competition: 1 (tough but differentiation possible)
- - Strategy clarity: -1.5 (B2B vs B2C unclear, pricing unclear)
- **= 7.0/10 (Indefinite Optimistic)**
