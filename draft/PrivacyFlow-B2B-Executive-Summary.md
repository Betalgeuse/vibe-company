## Critical Risk Register (Top 3)

### Risk 1: Sales Cycle Extension (40% probability, HIGH impact)
- **Scenario:** Legal/compliance approval takes 6-9 months, not 2-4
- **Impact:** CAC rises, customer velocity halves, cash flow tightens
- **Mitigation:** Identify budget owner early, emphasize regulatory deadline pressure

### Risk 2: CAC Inflation (50% probability, HIGH impact)
- **Scenario:** Real CAC $1.5K-2K (not $530) due to sales effort + market learning
- **Impact:** LTV:CAC drops from 63.5:1 to 15.75:1 (still acceptable)
- **Mitigation:** Build warm intro network (50+ privacy officers), establish partner channel Month 1

### Risk 3: Churn Rate High (30% probability, CRITICAL impact)
- **Scenario:** Customers churn 40%+ annually (compliance is cyclical, not ongoing)
- **Impact:** LTV drops $31.5K ‚Üí $8K, unit economics shift from exceptional ‚Üí acceptable
- **Mitigation:** Shift to "ongoing compliance service" model (monthly digest, quarterly training)

**Monitoring:** Track CAC, sales cycle, NRR weekly. Red flag if any metric deteriorates >20% vs plan.
