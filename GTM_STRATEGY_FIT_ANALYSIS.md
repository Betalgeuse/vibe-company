### 1.1: Can Onboarding Software Truly Be PLG?
**Reality Check**: ‚ùå **Limited PLG Viability**

**Evidence**:
- Rippling/BambooHR both have enterprise sales teams despite "simple" product
- Onboarding software requires integrations (HRIS, IT provisioning, background checks) ‚Üí SMBs need guidance
- Trial-to-paid conversion for HR SaaS = 5-10% (BambooHR benchmark)
- Free tier cannibalization risk: SMBs stuck on free tier longer than founders expect

**Why PLG Alone Fails**:
1. **Integration Complexity**: IT provisioning (Active Directory, Google Workspace, device management) not self-serve for most SMBs
2. **Buying Committee Friction**: VP People needs CFO approval for $8K+ ‚Üí can't self-serve alone
3. **Value Demonstration**: Trial needs real employee to onboard ‚Üí timing issue (not on-demand)
4. **Compliance Lock-in**: I-9/e-verify requires legal review ‚Üí forces sales conversation

**Verdict**: **PLG works for awareness/trial, NOT for conversion. Hybrid model required.**

### 1.4: Sales Cycle 45-90 Days - Realistic?
**Reality Check**: ‚úÖ **Realistic, but depends on GTM model**

**Competitor Sales Cycles**:
- **BambooHR** (self-serve dominant): 30-45 days (demo ‚Üí trial ‚Üí decision)
- **Rippling** (complex integrations): 90-180 days (discovery ‚Üí security review ‚Üí integration testing)
- **Gusto** (simple payroll): 14-30 days (sign-up ‚Üí onboarding)

**Your Product Sales Cycle Estimate**:
- **Discovery Call**: 1 week (founder outbound ‚Üí meeting booked)
- **Demo + Trial**: 2-3 weeks (they test with next batch of hires)
- **Integration Review**: 2-3 weeks (CFO, compliance, IT director involvement)
- **Legal/Security**: 1-2 weeks (SAML, I-9 compliance, data security)
- **Decision + Procurement**: 1-2 weeks (PO process, budget approval)

**Total**: 60-90 days (within target, heavy on evaluation)

**Factors That Extend Cycle**:
- Multiple stakeholders (VP People + CFO + IT director if IT provisioning required)
- Budget timing (quarterly/annual spending cycles)
- Security/compliance requirements (especially for IT provisioning)
- Switching cost from existing tool (data migration fear)

**Factors That Shorten Cycle**:
- Self-serve trial reducing discovery phase
- Clear pain point (high turnover, $420-600K cost quantified)
- Founder-led demo (high touch, faster relationship building)

**Verdict**: **45-90 days realistic IF you have founder-led sales + strong pain articulation. PLG-only = likely 90-120 days.**

### Critical Risk 2: ACV Drops to $5K-6K (Below Unit Economics)
**Risk**: Market pressure (BambooHR, Gusto competition) forces discounting; ACV drops below $6K

**Likelihood**: MEDIUM (50%)  
**Impact**: HIGH (breaks unit economics; CAC > LTV)

- If ACV = $6K ‚Üí LTV = $15K (at 2.5 year retention, 15% churn)
- If CAC = $1.5K ‚Üí CAC:LTV ratio = 1:10 ‚úÖ (healthy)
- If ACV drops to $5K ‚Üí LTV = $12.5K ‚Üí CAC:LTV ratio = 1:8.3 ‚úÖ (still okay)
- **Red line**: ACV < $5K ‚Üí CAC:LTV < 1:3 ‚ùå (unsustainable)

**Mitigation**:
1. ‚úÖ **Pricing discipline**: Never discount below $6K ACV (walk away from deals)
2. ‚úÖ **Value articulation**: Quantify $420-600K turnover savings ‚Üí justify $9K price
3. ‚úÖ **Upsell modules**: Post-onboarding AI support, offboarding, manager training (expand ACV to $12K+)
4. ‚úÖ **Segment up-market**: If downmarket too competitive, focus on Series A/B tech (higher budgets)

**Risk**: Free trial drives volume but conversion low; CAC creeps up due to low conversion rate
