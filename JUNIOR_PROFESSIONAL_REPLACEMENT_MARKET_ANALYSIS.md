### **GTM NOT Recommended**

**Why NOT 100% Product-Led Growth (PLG)?**
- ACV $15K-$30K is too high for pure PLG (requires sales)
- Law firms don't self-onboard easily (data sensitivity, integration, compliance)
- Free trials don't easily convert to paid (procurement required)
- Sales cycle is 2-4 months (too long for low-touch PLG)
- **Hybrid model is optimal**: Founder-led for first cohort, then product-led expansion

**Why NOT 100% Sales-Led Growth?**
- 2-4 month sales cycles are acceptable, but CAC is high
- Scaling sales reps isn't cost-effective until product is proven
- Once product has social proof (case studies), can shift to more product-led
