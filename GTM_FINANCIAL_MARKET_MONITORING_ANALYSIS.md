## PART 6: Risk & Mitigation Strategy

### Risk 1: Long Sales Cycle (9-12 months)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Impact** | Zero revenue for months 1-6; cash burn accelerates |
| **Severity** | **HIGH** ‚ö†Ô∏è |
| **Trigger** | If first deal closes after Month 9+ (beyond POC window) |

**Mitigation Strategies:**

1. **Pilot/POC Program ($0 or $5K discount)**
   - Action: Offer 4-week pilot to first 3-5 prospects
   - Benefit: Prove value within 4 weeks (not 9 months of discussions)
   - Downside: Lower revenue, but validates product
   - Timeline: Start Month 1-3

2. **Implementation Fees ($5-10K)**
   - Action: Charge setup fee (broker integration, compliance review)
   - Benefit: Early cash flow, even if subscription contract delayed
   - Timeline: Month 4-6

3. **Advisory/Consulting Engagement**
   - Action: Offer "Compliance Strategy for Market Monitoring" consulting ($10-20K)
   - Benefit: Revenue while main contract negotiates
   - Timeline: Months 2-8

**Expected Outcome:**
- Months 1-6: $0-15K from pilots + advisory
- Month 7-12: $30-60K from 1-2 main contracts
- Year 1 Total: $30-75K (vs. $120K base case)

### Risk 2: High Implementation Cost ($15-50K per customer)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Impact** | Negative gross margin in Year 1; NRR < 100% |
| **Severity** | **MEDIUM** ‚ö†Ô∏è |
| **Trigger** | If implementation takes 200+ hours per customer |

**Root Causes:**
- Broker API integration (custom for each broker)
- Compliance pre-flight and sign-off
- Data normalization (each broker has different schemas)
- Training and onboarding

**Mitigation Strategies:**

1. **Standardize Implementation (80% automation)**
   - Action: Build implementation playbooks for top 5 brokers (IB, Schwab, CenterPoint, Equinix, etc.)
   - Benefit: Reduce implementation from 200 hrs ‚Üí 50 hrs per customer
   - Timeline: Build during MVP phase, refine with first 2-3 customers
   - Cost: 200 hrs R&D = $40K (one-time)

2. **Partner Implementation**
   - Action: Partner with fund administration firms (Alter Domus, Equifax) for setup
   - Benefit: Offload implementation, improve customer satisfaction
   - Revenue Share: 20-30% of first year ACV
   - Timeline: Month 3-6

3. **Implementation Toolkit**
   - Action: Create self-service guides (YouTube, docs) for 80% of integration
   - Benefit: Reduce founder hand-holding from 150 hrs ‚Üí 50 hrs per customer
   - Timeline: Build during MVP, refine continuously

**Expected Impact:**
- Baseline implementation cost: $40-50K/customer
- Standardization target: $10-15K/customer (30% of ACV)
- Net: Improve gross margin from 60% ‚Üí 80%
- Timeline: Achieved by customer 4-5

### Risk 3: Regulatory Approval Delays (8-12 weeks)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Impact** | Sales cycle extends from 9-12 months ‚Üí 12-18 months |
| **Severity** | **MEDIUM** ‚ö†Ô∏è |
| **Trigger** | If compliance/legal review gets stuck (common in finance) |

**Regulatory Requirements:**
- SOC2 Type II certification (takes 6 months to audit, not 8-12 weeks of review)
- Form PF compliance verification (8-12 weeks, depends on SEC workload)
- Broker data agreements (legal review, 4-8 weeks)
- Customer agreement/SLA negotiation (2-4 weeks)

**Mitigation Strategies:**

1. **Pre-certifications (Quick Wins)**
   - Action: Achieve SOC2 Type II before first customer signs (Month 3-4)
   - Benefit: Removes single largest regulatory blocker
   - Cost: $15-25K audit fee
   - Timeline: Invest in Month 1-3 as priority

2. **Legal Pre-Work**
   - Action: Engage finance-focused law firm (Cooley, WilmerHale, etc.)
   - Benefit: Pre-drafted agreements, faster negotiations
   - Cost: $5-10K retainer
   - Timeline: Month 1

3. **Regulatory Advisor Relationship**
   - Action: Hire part-time Chief Compliance Officer (CCO) or advisor
   - Benefit: Internal + customer regulatory review speeds up
   - Cost: $2-5K/month retainer
   - Timeline: Months 1-2

4. **Pilot + Formal Contract Separation**
   - Action: Offer pilots under simple NDA (no compliance review needed)
   - Benefit: POC proceeds while contract negotiates
   - Timeline: Pilot: Weeks 1-4 | Formal contract: Weeks 5-24

**Expected Impact:**
- Without mitigation: First contract Month 12+
- With mitigation: First contract Month 6-9
- Savings: 3-4 months acceleration
