## Your Discovery Call Script (30 min)

### Opening (2 min)
```
"Thanks for jumping on. I'm [Name], building PSA for consulting firms. 
What's your current role at [company]?"
```

### Problem Discovery (10 min)
```
"Tell me about your current process for time tracking and billing."
[Listen for pain: manual spreadsheets, hours spent, errors, team frustration]

"What's the biggest pain point with your current setup?"
[Quantify: hours/week wasted, $$ cost, team frustration level]

"How long does month-end close take you?"
[Target: >5 days = clear pain]
```

### Qualification (10 min)
```
"Are you actively looking for a better solution?" 
[Flag: If "just researching", lower priority]

"What would the right tool need to do for you?"
[Identify must-haves vs nice-to-haves]

"Who else should be in a conversation about this? You're the champion‚Äîwho decides?"
[Identify VP/CFO if not already talking to them]

"If the right tool saved your team 10 hours/week, what would that be worth?"
[Price sensitivity check: >$15K/year = good signal]

"What's your timeline for a decision?"
[Flag: >3 months = likely stalls]
```

### Close (3 min)
```
If qualified: "Here's what I propose: let me do a quick 45-minute demo 
tailored to your setup. Show you exactly how we'd help. Does [day] work?"

If not qualified: "Thanks for your time. If things change, reach out. 
Happy to help."
```

### Qualification Score:
‚úÖ = 4+ of these:
- Admitting manual pain (spreadsheets, hours spent)
- Have budget ($15K+/year)
- Team size 10-200
- Decision cycle <3 months
- Champion identified

For a 25-person firm, DeliverySync is simpler, faster to implement, 40% cheaper.

customers similar to them.] Any specific concerns?"

### "Implementation looks complicated"
**Response:**
"For a firm your size, 2-4 weeks. We handle data migration. You focus on 
setup rules. I'll assign a dedicated success manager to guide you."

### "Can you replicate our Excel setup?"
**Response:**
"Honestly? Not perfectly‚Äîyour setup is very custom. But we automate the 80% 
that's tedious (spreadsheet management, calculations, updates). You might lose 
the 20% custom stuff. Is that worth 10 hours/week saved?"

## Trial Success Metrics (How to Close Trials)

**By Day 5:**
- Team invited to DeliverySync
- First 10 hours logged
- One project imported

**By Day 15 (Critical Check-in):**
- Call customer: "How's it going?"
- Target: 50+ hours logged, team actively using
- If NOT: Help troubleshoot immediately. This is make-or-break.

**By Day 25 (Final Push):**
- Utilization report auto-generating (proves value)
- Customer should say "Wow, I can see exactly who's billable"
- If YES ‚Üí Close conversation: "Ready to move forward?"

**If Trial Flops (<20 hours logged by day 15):**
- This is a PRODUCT issue, not sales issue
- Call customer: "We noticed limited usage. What's blocking you?"
- Don't push to paid. Fix the blocker. Learn for next customer.
